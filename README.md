**Learn about business, stocks, and the world—made just for kids (ages 10-16)**

## 🎯 Getting Started

### 1. First Launch

When you open News To Me for the first time:
- Enter your name
- Select your age (10-16)
- Choose your country
- That's it! The app will generate news for you

### 2. Choose Your AI Provider

News To Me uses AI to generate news summaries. You need to choose one provider:

**Free Options:**
- **Google Gemini** - Free tier available (60 requests/min)
- **xAI (Grok)** - Free tier available

**Paid Options (Most Affordable):**
- **OpenAI** - $5/month free credits, then pay-as-you-go
- **Anthropic** - Pay-as-you-go pricing
- **Cohere** - Free tier with limits

### 3. Get an API Key

**For Google Gemini (Recommended - Free):**
1. Go to https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
2. Click "Create API Key"
3. Copy the key
4. Paste it into News To Me

**For xAI (Recommended - Free):**
1. Go to https://x.ai/api
2. Sign up
3. Create an API key
4. Paste it into News To Me

**For OpenAI:**
1. Go to https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys
2. Click "Create new secret key"
3. Copy and paste into News To Me

### 4. Read Your News!

- Browse the newspaper pages
- Tap stories to learn more
- Swipe or click arrows to navigate pages
- Click "Regenerate" to get new stories

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Is the app free?
**A:** The app is free! You only pay for the AI service you choose (or use free tiers).

### Q: Is my information safe?
**A:** Yes! Your API key is encrypted with military-grade security. We never store or send your data anywhere.

### Q: How often are stories updated?
**A:** Every time you launch the app or click "Regenerate", News To Me fetches fresh stories from the internet and generates new summaries.

### Q: Can I use it without internet?
**A:** The app needs internet to fetch news and generate summaries, but it runs entirely on your computer (not in the cloud).
